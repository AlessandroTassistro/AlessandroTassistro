Graphic designer at Combo

WHAT IS COMBO?
Combo is a modern hostel chain where art meets a new idea of hospitality. During my time there I was one of the three main representatives of the marketing department. My duties regarded mainly the production and diffusion of graphic supports used by the four different locations based in Torino, Milano, Venezia, and Bologna.
EVENT MAKING AND ADVERTISING
As part of the marketing department, I helped with the creation and advertising of exhibitions, parties, and Dj sets. Talking with artists, musicians, and yoga instructors was only part of my job, I was also in charge of the making of the supports used to promote the events.
SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGMENT
The making of an editorial plan and copywriting were the main tasks I followed regarding the brand's social network. Combo is a place where everybody is welcomed, the same can be said for its accounts. We used a friendly and engaging tone of voice putting the spotlight on the beautiful spaces and the enjoyable events that take place every day.
IMAGES PRODUCTION
In an always-changing environment like Combo a camera was essential to capture new plates, and friendly faces. I also usually helped during photo shooting where I decorated the different environments.
SIMPLE AND BIG TASKS
Being the only graphic designer I was the sole responsible for the production of simple elements like restaurant menus and merchandising. In the meantime, I kept updating the already existing brand guidelines in order to keep up with the latest trends and innovations. The biggest task I was assigned was the complete renovation of the latest location's wayfinding, where I worked in collaboration with the external design studio “LeftLoft”.
