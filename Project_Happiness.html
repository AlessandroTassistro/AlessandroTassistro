Progetto Happiness rebrand

THE PROJECT
For my Social Media and Content Management class at IAAD, I had the task to revamp the social identity of one of my favorite social media influencers, Progetto Happiness. Created by Giuseppe Bertuccio D'Angelo, a 32-year-old originally from Messina, Progetto Happiness embodies a journey of personal and professional growth through travel, discovery, and relationships.
THE CHANNEL IN NUMBERS
THE SWOT
The initial step in revitalizing the social identity for Progetto Happiness was to thoroughly understand the existing channels used for content dissemination. To achieve this, I conducted a comprehensive SWOT analysis—assessing the Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats associated with the current social media strategy. The SWOT analysis provided a clear framework to recognize potential opportunities for growth, such as expanding into new social media platforms or incorporating more diverse content formats, and highlighted external threats like competitive channels.

THE COMPETITORS
As part of the project to revamp the social identity of Progetto Happiness, I conducted an extensive competitor analysis, focusing on both the Italian market and the international scene. This analysis involved a dive into the strategies employed by other channels that resonate within the same thematic areas of travel, personal growth, and cultural discovery. By examining these competitors, I was able to assess how they engage with their audiences, the types of content they produce, and the platforms they prioritize.

After scrutinizing these various channels, my goal was to identify market vacancies—opportunities where Progetto Happiness could uniquely position itself to capture audience attention.

Starting from the insights collected from the first part of the analysis we defined the two main goals of the project:

THE NEW CREATIVE CONCEPT AND TOV
After identifying market opportunities, the next step was to develop a new creative concept for Progetto Happiness, refining the tone of voice and devising a diverse content mix.

The existing tone of voice for Project Happiness is clearly defined and effective; however, it requires standardization across all touchpoints.

Project Happiness pages are envisioned as welcoming spaces where everyone feels safe and accepted. Therefore, the tone of voice should be friendly, resembling the one used by an old friend.

Simultaneously, Project Happiness should strive to maintain its current journalistic approach to longer storytelling.

THE VODCAST
The main introduction of the content plan is the vodcast, which will be available on YouTube and Spotify.

- Storytime Sessions:
 This is the first part of the vodcast dedicated to storytelling, featuring heartwarming anecdotes, personal journeys, and transformative experiences shared by Giuseppe. Encourage audience participation by inviting submissions of their own stories.

- The Happiness Experiment:
 A recurring segment where the host and guests embark on happiness experiments based on scientific research and positive psychology.

- The Happiness Hot Seat: “VICE risponde”:
 A segment where listeners can submit their happiness-related questions, dilemmas, and struggles. The funniest and curious questions will be selected and given to Giuseppe to answer them.

- The Joyful Playlist:
 Themed episodes centered around curated playlists designed to uplift and energize listeners. Explore the role of music in enhancing mood and well-being, featuring guest DJs, musicians, and traditional musicians from all over the world who share their favorite tracks and the stories behind them.

THE NEW NEWSLETTER
Given the low popularity of the blog and the inactivity of the newsletter, I decided to merge the two tools into one.

 The newsletter will be issued monthly and it will cover various topics, primarily focusing on expanding upon the content of the previous week's videos’ and offering a preview of the upcoming content.

The newsletter will adopt a traditional newspaper format, featuring columns on recent events, food, travel, and technology, all unified by the central theme of happiness.

THE NEW LINKEDIN
LinkedIn will serve as a platform for Project Happiness to engage with other brands and creators for potential collaborations or to showcase partnerships. There will be two primary types of content:

- Sponsor-related content:
 The first type of content is dedicated to acknowledging and thanking the sponsors and partners of Project Happiness. It will detail their contributions and the projects they have supported. This section may also feature interviews with sponsors, discussing their reasons for supporting the mission of Project Happiness and the significance of promoting global happiness.

- Infographic regarding data collected by the project:
 SThe second type of content will be a data-driven column that presents infographics and analyses related to happiness metrics from around the globe. It will explore trends, scientific discoveries, and socio-economic factors that influence happiness levels worldwide and regionally. This column aims to offer a visually engaging and insightful examination of what contributes to societies thriving while redirecting the Project Happiness’ content.
