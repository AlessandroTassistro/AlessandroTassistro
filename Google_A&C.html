Google A&C curator

WHAT IS GOOGLE A&C?
During the coordination of the fourth edition of Graphic Days Torino we were contacted by the Italian department of Google Arts & Culture, a platform completely dedicated to the docu- mentation and sharing of art in all of its forms.
ADVERTISING AND DOCUMENTATION
Being an always changing and updaded place Google Arts & Culture is the perfect way to narrate an event. All the past editions of the festival were documented and reported on the platform in order to make it possible to live again the festival everywhere and every time the digital visitor wants.
MATERIAL DOCUMENTATION
Working closely with Google affiliates all the content of the festival was documented. The focal task was to collect enough material to create a complete narration of the different days of the festival, this task was achieved in different ways such as taking high-fidelity pictures of every piece in every exhibition and recording both audio and video of all the performances and talks that happened.
EXHIBITIONS AND TALKS CURATION
In the period following the end of the festival, I was one of the two people that curated the uploading of all the material on the platform. Through the tools available on the website we were able to create several digital exhibitions and on-demand talks.
